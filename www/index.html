Contribute

Thank you for wanting to contribute to "visitanvil.com"! Currently, two main contribution methods exist; "Point of Interest" and "Area" (explained below). All submissions should have a direct link to a Empire WiKi page since I can then automatically extract information such the locations name, description etc.

Submitting a sub-header on a wikipedia article is also completely fine, just remember to include the sub-headers ID in the url (nearly always just the headers name with underscores instead of spaces), for instance the link to "The Red Blade Memorial" in Skarsind is https://www.profounddecisions.co.uk/empire-wiki/Skarsind#The_Red_Blade_Memorial. You will be prompted for the link; please check it before submitting.

A "Point of Interest" is a single location on the map, like a mine or a town. These are displayed as markers. A "Point of Interest" is the easiest to submit. After entering the Empire WiKi link, you just select a single point on the map (clicking again replaces your choise, so just keep clicking until you are satisfied) and you are done.

An area is shown as coloured polygons on the map and represents areas such as regions or nations. After entering the Empire WiKi link, you will create a polygon by clicking its corners in order around the map. If you hold in "control" while clicking, it will snap to the nearest corner of an existing area. Be careful; there is no undo button (yet)!

When you are done "drawing on the map" just click the contribution button on the left (the one you clicked to get this popup) again to submit. The website will open your email client with an email ready to send. This email contains all information I need, but feel free to add/extend the message or anything you think is missing.

If you have found an error on the current map or have a good idea, please email admin@visitanvil.com.
