Contribute

Thank you for wanting to contribute to "visitanvil.com"! Currently, two main contribution methods exist; "Point of Interest" and area, they can be selected by clicking the buttons below.

A "Point of Interest" is a single location on the map, like a mine or a town. These are displayed as markers. A "Point of Interest" is the easiest to submit. After you have filled out the information about the location, you select a single point on the map and you are done.

An area is shown as coloured polygons on the map and represents areas such as regions or nations. After filling in the information about the area, you will create a polygon by clicking its corners in order around the map. If you hold in "control" while clicking, it will snap to the nearest corner of an existing area. Be careful; there is no undo button yet!

All contributions are sent over email to the admin. The website will open your email client with an email ready to send when you finish your contribution. This email contains all information needed by the admin, but feel free to add messages or anything you think is missing.

If you have found an error on the current map or have a good idea, please email admin@visitanvil.com.
